About Me

I am a rising senior at UMass Lowell. I am currently studying computer science and am primarily interested in software engineering. I love everything tech, finance, and soccer! I am originally from Danvers, Massachussetts where I grew up most of my life. From a young age, I always gravitated towards electronics. As I grew older, my father gave me a computer and the rest was history. I attended coding classes at my library and took a strong interest in software development. Coupled with my obsession with video games, I started to dabble in game development. Nearing the end of my high school years, I decided to attend UMass Lowell as a computer science student with the main goal of building the foundational knowledge required so that I could become competent in the software engineering industry.
